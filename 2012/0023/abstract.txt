I will create a Joomla Platform package which implements a subset of Google APIs. I tentatively recommend the following APIs: Google OAuth/ClientLogin, AdSense Management, Google Analytics Tracking, Google Calendar, Google Calendar Embed, Google Maps, Google Picasa, reCAPTCHA Embed, and Google Short Links. In addition to these APIs, I will write generic, extensible classes which connect to Google via OAuth and other authentication protocols to send/receive data.
