The world we are living in is overwhelmed by the spread of numerous diseases. They can be hard to cure and some might even be life threatening. This is even a greater problem in developing countries where the governments do not have money in abundance to invest in state-of-the-art health care management systems. In this context, OpenMRS can be a life saver for the poor countries that rely on stone aged systems to manage information in their hospital systems. In this proposal, I will be presenting how I plan on making the patient dashboard AJAX enabled so that it loads quickly and easily.
