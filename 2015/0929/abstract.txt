the p5.js flavor of Processing is designed to provide an easy environment for novice programmers, designers and artists to develop for the web. I want to work on improving the p5.js editor in two capacities: Fixing known issues with the current version of the p5.js editor (currently in Mac-only alpha), and by developing Windows and Linux versions the editor so that a larger number of aspiring digital artists and programmers will have reliable, easy access to p5.js through its IDE.
