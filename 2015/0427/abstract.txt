Entity browser is a contrib module for Drupal 8 that tries to provide powerful and flexible framework for searching & selecting of entities. There are many possible use cases that entity browser wants to solve:
-select an image to be embedded in WYSIWYG editor
-select few related content items & "attach" them to another content
-upload few images then select few more from library of existing media & use all of them in a slideshow
-search YouTube for videos & attach them to a piece of content
