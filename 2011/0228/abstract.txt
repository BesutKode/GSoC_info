This project consistst of two parts.

The first part will produce a generic and powerfull Right Click Menu (also known as Context Menu) component for Tapestry5. The Right Click Menu component is meant to be highly configurable ready for enterprise scenarios.

The second part will provide enhancements for the existing powerful t5 grid component: bookmarkable URLs with sort parameters of the grid, page number and number of items per page.

Also two optional tasks are proposed.
