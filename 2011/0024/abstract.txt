OpenMRS is an open source EMR, with its own method of extension through modules.Likewise all EMRs have their own way of extending their functionality.This is annoying for a developer,He has to develop a couple of applications to all possible EMR to ensure that his idea reaches to all the users regardless of what EMR they are using.The SMART platform provides a method to develop applications that can run within any EMR.The purpose of this project is to enable SMART capabilities in OpenMRS.
